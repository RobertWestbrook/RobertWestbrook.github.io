Antioch Mobile

Project information

  • Category: Mobile Development
  • Client: Antioch Community Church
  • Project date: August, 2020
  • Project URL: https://github.com/RobertWestbrook/Antioch-Mobile

"The Antioch Mobile app is a fully cross platform app whose sole purpose is to provide a mobile solution to better foster communication and connection with a local church congregation."

This app is really a sort of debut for me. I have never before attempted to do mobile development; however, I work really well when I see purpose in what I am doing. Using my knowledge of programming and shear grit and determination, I learned Flutter and went to town. In less than a month I learned the language and built the solid MVP that you see here.

Tech Stack Used:

  • Flutter

  • Firebase
